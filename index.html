Empowering Businesses in Oyo State

Driving Economic Growth and Investment Opportunities

At the Ministry of Trade, Industry, Investment, and Cooperatives, we are committed to creating an environment where businesses thrive.

By fostering industrial development, supporting local enterprises, and attracting global investors, we pave the way for sustainable growth in Oyo State.

Why Oyo State

Oyo State offers an unmatched blend of rich culture, strategic location, and thriving industries, making it the ideal destination for business growth and investment. With a government dedicated to fostering innovation and supporting entrepreneurs, Oyo State is committed to creating a conducive environment for both local and international traders, exporters and investors.

Whether you're looking to tap into key sectors like agriculture, infrastructure, or technology, Oyo State provides diverse opportunities, coupled with attractive incentives and policies designed to drive success. Join us in shaping a prosperous future in one of Nigeria's most dynamic regions.

Our Focus

  • Strategy & Research

    Our Strategy & Research team provides in-depth insights and data-driven analysis to guide investment decisions and policy formulation. By understanding market trends and identifying opportunities, we help businesses and investors navigate the evolving economic landscape of Oyo State with confidence.

  • Industrial Development

    We are dedicated to driving industrial growth by fostering innovation, supporting manufacturing, and enhancing infrastructure. Oyo State offers a robust framework for industries to thrive, ensuring sustainable development and economic advancement for the region.

  • Business Solutions

    From business registration to operational support, our tailored solutions empower enterprises to succeed. Whether you're a startup or an established organization, we provide the resources and expertise you need to grow and make a lasting impact in Oyo State.

Our Services

  • Trade Support

    We simplify the complexities of trade by offering comprehensive support services, including business registration, trade facilitation, and market access. Our goal is to empower local businesses to thrive and compete effectively in both domestic and global markets.

  • Quality Assurance

    Ensuring excellence is at the heart of what we do. Our quality assurance programs help businesses meet industry standards, gain certifications, and deliver products and services that inspire trust and confidence in local and international markets.

  • Investment Promotion

    We connect investors with opportunities that matter. Through strategic initiatives and personalized guidance, we attract and support investments that drive growth across key sectors, making Oyo State a hub of innovation and economic prosperity.

  • Export Promotion

    Expand your reach with our tailored export promotion programs. From readiness assessments to export documentation, we equip businesses with the tools and resources needed to enter global markets and showcase the best of Oyo State to the world.
